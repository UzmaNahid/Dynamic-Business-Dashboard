# Dynamic-Business-Sales-Dashboard
## Objective
Built a dynamic business of Bars and Chocolates dashboard in Excel using pivot tables, slicers, formulas, and interactive charts. Analyzed sales, profit %, boxes sold, and salesperson performance across multiple products and regions. The dashboard enables quick visibility into key metrics, trends, and top-performing sales staff.

## Questions(KPI)
1. What is the total sales generated during the selected period?

2. How many boxes were sold overall?

3. What is the overall profit % and how is it changing?

4. Which products perform the best or worst?

5. Which salesperson contributed the most to revenue?

6. Which product categories show growth or decline over time?

7. What are the month-wise trends in sales and boxes sold?

8. How does performance change when selecting different filters (Product, Region, Category)?
